## Overview

Realtime Observability Suite centralizes logs, metrics, and traces for product and infrastructure workloads.

## Key Capabilities

- Unified dashboard for service health and latency
- Alert routing to on-call channels
- Incident timeline with correlated events
- SLO tracking for critical APIs

## Tech Stack

- ClickHouse, Prometheus, Loki
- OpenTelemetry collectors
- React + TypeScript frontend

## Outcome

The suite shortened incident detection and improved triage efficiency by providing one consistent operational view.
